## Ingredients:
- salt, pepper
- Garlic powder/salt
- Bbq sauce
- Paprika
- Ribs

## Cooking:
- Generously season with the spices/salt/pepper
- Oven to 425F
- Cook 25 mins concave down, 15 up, 20+ up
- Total 1hr - 1:15
- Remove, dowse in bbq
- Cover/seal in foil, oven to 250F
- 2 hours in the oven covered
