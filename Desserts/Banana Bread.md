## Ingredients:
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup crushed drained pineapple
- 4 bananas mashed
- 2 cups flour
- 1 tbsp. Baking soda
- 1 tsp. Baking powder
- 1 tsp. Cinnamon
- 1/2 cup raisins
- 1/2 cup walnuts
- 1/2 cup oil - olive oil, weed oil, etc.

## Baking:
- Put in greased & floured bundt pan or
- 50-55 min @ 350
